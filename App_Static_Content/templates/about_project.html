About The Neurodegenerative Disorder Pathology Database

What is the Problem?

  • Neurodegenerative disorders are complex diseases with an interlinking of multiple environmental and genetic risk factors which subsequently complicates risk communication and early intervention efforts.
  • The dynamism of risk factors necessitates a comprehensive database that can track risk factors contributing to neurodegenerative disease pathology.

What Gaps does the NDPD Fill?

  • Addresses the challenge of limited accessibility for users without experience in the medical or scientific field.
  • Acts as a bridge between the advancing literature of neurodegenerative diseases and those wishing to investigate their risk for neurodegenerative diseases.

Our Goal

Provide an accessible and user-friendly database that comprehensively connects multiclass risk factors for neurodegenerative disorders with current information.
