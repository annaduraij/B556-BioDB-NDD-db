NDD DB
Advantages
Multiclass Linkages
NDD DB collects and spans information from academic articles for environmental factors linked to diseases and also links diseases with SNP information for genes, providing a holistic view of neurodegenerative disorder mechanisms across several diseases.
Accessibility
NDD DB features a simple user interface designed to facilitate quick and robust searches across three main factors (disease, genetic, and environmental). The system is geared towards delivering information in a readable and easily interpretable format.
Scalability
NDD DB's data architecture and data acquisition methods are both streamlined and provide flexibility for future expansion. Multitudes of diseases and their linked genetic and environmental factors may be imported via manual curation or by data scraping models.
